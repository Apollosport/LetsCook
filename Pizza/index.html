Welcome Pizza Lovers

Better than delivery, every time! Here are the best pizza recipes, with top-rated pizza dough, sauces, and topping ideas galore.

Pizza Napoletana

Italys most emblematic culinary creation, the genuine pizza Napoletana is made with just a few simple ingredients and prepared in only two variations – marinara, the basic Neapolitan pizza topped with a tomato-based sauce flavored with garlic and oregano.

Pizza Margherita

Pizza margherita, as the Italians call it, is a simple pizza hailing from Naples. When done right, margherita pizza features a bubbly crust, crushed San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella and basil, a drizzle of olive oil.

Pizza Siciliana

Sicilian pizza, also known as sfincione (“thick sponge” in Italian), is a thick-crust, rectangular pizza that is similar to focaccia bread. The thick, spongy crust distinguishes the pizza from thin-crust Neapolitan pizza, Roman pizza, and New York–style pizza.

Pizza Inglesina

LPizza inglesina is a variety of traditional Italian pizza that is topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and prosciutto crudo. This last ingredient is a type of traditional Italian raw, dry-cured ham as opposed to prosciutto cotto, which is cooked ham.

Pizza Boscaiola

Boscaiola is a traditional Italian pizza variety that is topped with mozzarella, porcini or champignon mushrooms, tomatoes, cream, and sliced Italian sausage. It is occasionally prepared without tomatoes and can be enriched with a drizzle of olive oil, olives.

Pizza Parmigiana

Inspired by another Italian dish - the traditional Southern Italian specialty, known as parmigiana di melanzane or eggplant parmesan - pizza parmigiana is a tasty twist on this dish that uses the dish's main ingredients as toppings.
